Our Story

"Air Jordy is a brand of basketball shoes created for Michael Jordy during his time with the Chic Balls. The original Air Jordan sneakers were produced exclusively for Michael Jordy in late 1984, and released to the public on April 1, 1985. In 1995, the Air Jordy XI burst onto the basketball scene, marking a new era in athletic footwear. Crafted with innovative materials and design elements, these shoes offered unmatched performance and style. With their sleek silhouette and advanced features, they quickly became a symbol of excellence on and off the court. Endorsed by basketball legend Michael Jordan, the Air Jordy XI captured the hearts of athletes and fashion enthusiasts alike, solidifying its place in sneaker history as a true icon of innovation and style.
